Feeds

Feeds are patterns of at least 3 jugglers. Normally one juggler is declared has the "feeder" (there could be multiple feeders) who’s job is the feed objects all other juggler. All "feedees" generally only exchange objects with the feeder.

Two-Person, 6 Clubs

S - Self
D - Diagonal Pass

B - Slow Fast Fast Slow

A: PS DS | S | P D | S | P D | S | PS DS | S |

B: P D | S | PS DS | S | PS DS | S | P D | S |
Notes:
  1. A s PSDS takes place during the same time frame as B s PD so A needs to speed up a little (that s why it is called the fast part) and B needs to slow down a little (hence, the slow position). B s passes would be lofty but still a single spin)
  2. After the transitional self ( | S | ) it is the Fast Slow pattern on the other side.
  3. You might also try replacing the transitional self with a Reading Romp.
  4. Instead of repeating after finishing, you could switch positions at the end and so on ... Or switch to another pattern ... of course you have to have agreed to what you will switch!?!?

Alternating Box Feed

Four Person, 12 Clubs

C B

A D
A and B start feeding ... That is, A feeds C and B (starting with C) and B feeds D and A (Starting with D). After a complete cycle (two passes), C and D take over feeding.

A - CSBS CSSS
B - DSAS DSSS
C - ASSS ASDS
D - BSSS BSCS

Once everyone is comfortable with the above try adding Ho-Hos!

Alternating Box Feed with Ho-Hos on the Diagonal

Four Person, 12 Clubs

C B

A D
A and B start feeding ... That is, A feeds C and B (starting with C) and B feeds D and A (Starting with D). It is a standard two-count feed except after the pass along the diagonal (A and B or C and D) follow with a left pass. After a complete cycle (two passes and the ho-ho), C and D take over feeding.
A - CSBB CSSS
B - DSAA DSSS
C - ASSS ASDD
D - BSSS BSCC
Note: A Ho-Ho is an extra pass not associated with the base pattern. A Pre-Ho is an extra pass immediately before a normal pass and a Post-Ho is an extra pass immediately after a normal pass.

Dave and Ralph's Endless Patter Pattern

Two or More People (People Can Come And Go At Will - And They Usually Do)

Any Number Of Clubs (It Is Important To Have Some Clubs So That People Coming Into The "Pattern" Think That Juggling Will Be Starting Soon, But Under No Circumstances Should Any Clubs Be Thrown While Doing This Pattern)

P - Straight Pass

D - Diagonal Pass (Could Also Mean Double - And Sometimes means Double-Diagonal)

S - Self (or single)

H - Hand Across (also hold, hurry, or hairbrained-idea)

N - Nod (as in agreement - usually by two or more people agreeing to go somewhere else after they realize that the patter is actually the pattern)

A - Argument (We are not argling!)

NC - Notation Change (It is important to change the meaning of notation in the middle of the conversation)

T - Transition, Taxing, Tempting, ...

Right Handed Start (Usually - Sometimes You Can Start In The Middle Of The Pattern and Then You Might Start With The Left Hand)

Now Try Any Combination Of The Above.
